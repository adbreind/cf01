Lumberville Market Square

The town's central gathering place is getting more comfortable, more flexible, and just a little more irresistible.

A square for every season

Market Square improvements will create a flexible civic space for farmers markets, concerts, winter lights, outdoor dining, and everyday meetups under a healthy canopy of new shade trees.

Public space upgrades

  • Level plaza paving for markets, events, and easier accessibility.
  • More seating, drinking fountains, and power for vendors.
  • Stormwater planters filled with native flowers and grasses.
  • Warm pedestrian lighting that flatters both storefronts and selfies.
