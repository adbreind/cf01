Lumberville International Airport

A smarter regional gateway for visitors, residents, and the occasional suitcase that packed itself.

Taking off thoughtfully

The airport improvement program focuses on right-sized service: a more efficient terminal, better ground transportation, quieter operations, and a warm first impression rooted in Lumberville's timber-and-river character.

Planned improvements

  • Expanded arrivals hall with local wood finishes and clearer wayfinding.
  • Electric shuttle bays linking the terminal, rail station, and downtown.
  • Noise-conscious scheduling and updated runway lighting.
  • New viewing terrace for plane fans and patient coffee drinkers.
