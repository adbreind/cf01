Lumberville Rail Terminal

A riverfront station that makes commuting calmer, visiting easier, and timetable checking almost fun.

All aboard, without the scramble

The updated terminal will bring sheltered platforms, safer pedestrian crossings, improved bus and bike connections, and a station plaza that welcomes travelers directly onto the riverwalk.

What changes

  • Accessible platforms with generous weather protection.
  • Bike lockers, repair stands, and a brighter kiss-and-ride area.
  • Coordinated shuttle service to the airport and Market Square.
  • Riverwalk landscaping that turns waiting into lingering.

Designed for daily life

Morning commuters, students, visitors, and downtown businesses all benefit when the terminal works as a civic front porch instead of a place everyone speed-walks through.
